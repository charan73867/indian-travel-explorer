I'm planning a trip to India in November. Which places would you recommend?
November is a perfect time to visit India! The weather is pleasant in most regions. I'd recommend Delhi, Rajasthan, and Agra (Golden Triangle) for history and culture. Kerala in the south for natural beauty, or Goa for beaches. What interests you most: culture, nature, or relaxation?
I'm interested in culture and history. How many days should I plan for the Golden Triangle?
For a comfortable Golden Triangle experience (Delhi-Agra-Jaipur), I recommend 7-8 days. This allows:

2-3 days in Delhi exploring Old & New Delhi
1-2 days in Agra for Taj Mahal & Agra Fort
2-3 days in Jaipur for palaces, forts & local culture
